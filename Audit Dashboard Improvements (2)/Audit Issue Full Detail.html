Offending Element (first instance)
<img src="/wiki/logo.png"
  class="site-logo">
Images must have text alternatives to convey meaning to screen reader users. 12 images on this page have no alt attribute or have an empty string that hides informative content.
Remediation guidance
Add a descriptive alt attribute to all informative images. For purely decorative images, use alt="" to hide them from assistive technology. Never omit the attribute entirely.
